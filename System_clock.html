HSI (High-speed Internal) RC Oscillator

  1. The HSI is used (enabled by hardware) as system clock source after startup from reset, wake-up from STOP and STANDBY mode, or in case of failure of the HSE used directly or indrectly s system clock.

  2. The HSI RC oscillator has the advantage of providing a clock source at loe cost (no external components).

  3. It also has a faster startup(2usec) time than the HSE crystal oscillator.

  4. However even with calibration the frequency is less accurate than an external crystal osillator or ceramic resonator.

  5. The HSI signal can also be used as a backup source (auxiliary clock) if the HSE crystal oscillator fails.

HSI calibration

The operating temperature has an impact on the accuracy of the RC oscillator.At 25decC, the HSI oscillator have an accuracy of +/- 1% typically,but in the temperature range okf -40 degC to 105 degC the accuracy decreases.
To compensate for the influence of temperature in the application the O/P frequency of the HSI oscillator can be further trimmed by the user runtime calibration routine to improve the HSI frequency accuracy.This may rove crucial for communication peripherals.
